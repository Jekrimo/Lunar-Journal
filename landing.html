What is Lunations
A lunar journal.
Your inner weather, mapped.
A lunation is one complete cycle of the moon — 29.5 days of inner weather. Most apps show you the sky. Lunations helps you see yourself through the sky. Track your energy, mood, clarity, and creativity across all 13 cycles of the year. Over time, your patterns emerge.
Features
🌑
Daily Entry
Four-metric check-in — energy, mood, clarity, creativity — with quality tags, reflection, and an evening closing entry.
✦
AI Sky Readings
Daily personalized readings based on your natal chart, current transits, and the actual sky. Not generic horoscopes.
📅
13-Cycle Calendar
The year as 13 lunar cycles, not 12 months. Navigate your history by cycle, see energy bars for every day.
◈
Pattern Analysis
Over time, see which moon phases are your peak periods and which ones challenge you. Your personal lunar fingerprint.
🌍
Live Space Weather
Real-time Kp index, solar wind speed, and geomagnetic activity. The electromagnetic field is part of your day too.
🕉
Six Tradition Modes
Vedic, Goddess, Crystal, Elemental, Nature, or Source. Choose the lens through which the sky speaks to you.
